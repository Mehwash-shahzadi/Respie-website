Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • sugar
  • Bread
  • oil

Respie

  1. Take 1-cup sugar, 3 green cardamom pods, 1½ cups water and 1-2 drops of lemon juice in a deep pot or pan.
  2. Heat it over medium flame and cook until it reaches 1/2 string consistency or turns little sticky.
  3. Take 1/4 cup milk powder, 1/4 cup maida and 1/8 teaspoon baking soda in a wide mouthed bowl.
  4. Mix it well with a spoon and then add 1-tablespoon ghee.
